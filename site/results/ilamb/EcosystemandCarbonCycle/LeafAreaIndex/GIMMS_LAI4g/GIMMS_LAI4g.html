  Title:
Spatiotemporally consistent global dataset of the GIMMS Leaf Area Index (GIMMS LAI4g) from 1982 to 2020

  Institution:

  Source:
The GIMMS LAI4g was generated based on biome-specific BPNN models that employed the latest PKU GIMMS NDVI product and 3.6 million high-quality global Landsat LAI samples. It was then consolidated with the Reprocess MODIS LAI to extend the temporal coverage to 2020 via a pixel-wise Random Forests fusion method.

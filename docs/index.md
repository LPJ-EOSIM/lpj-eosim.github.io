# Welcome to LPJ-Earth Observation SIMulator (LPJ-EOSIM)

LPJ-EOSIM is a process-based dynamic global vegetation model (DGVM). It
simulates terrestrial vegetation dynamics together with the carbon, water, and
nitrogen cycles, fire, wetland methane, and land use, on a global 0.5° grid at
daily-to-annual timesteps.

The model builds on the LPJ / LPJ-wsl lineage (Sitch et al. 2003; Bondeau et
al. 2007), and has since included many improvments, including a representation of permafrost,
wetland methane, nitrogen cycling and associated trace gases, surface reflectance, and an updated fire module (
SPITFIRE).
